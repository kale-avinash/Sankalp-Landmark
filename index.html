What is Sankalp Landmark?
Sankalp Landmark is a trusted real estate company that offers end-to-end property solutions from planning to final sale.
Where are your projects located?
Our projects are spread across various parts of Maharashtra, including both urban and rural areas. Please visit the Projects section for details.
Are the plots legally clear?
Yes, all our plots are 100% legally verified with complete documents like NA, 7/12 extract, and property clearance certificates.
How can I book a plot?
You can book a plot by contacting us through the website or by visiting our office in person.
What documents are required for booking?
You’ll need your Aadhaar card, PAN card, and a passport-size photo to complete the booking.

Yes, we offer assistance with home loans through trusted banks and finance companies.

Absolutely! Just get in touch with us to book a free site visit at your convenience.

Plot prices vary depending on the project location and available amenities. Please contact us for detailed pricing.
Do you assist with property registration?
Yes, we help with the entire registration process and ensure all legal procedures are handled smoothly.
What amenities are available in the projects?
Our projects include amenities like internal roads, water supply, street lights, plot numbering, compound wall, and more.
